## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Geographic Footprint** — The total count of unique countries and regions present in the dataset.
- **Market Penetration** — The extent of coverage based on the number of unique states and cities reached.

## 📈 Key Metrics
### Geographic Distribution
- **Country Coverage** — Total count of unique countries represented.
- **Regional Representation** — Total count of unique broad regions.
- **State Count** — Number of unique states included in the data.

### Operational Granularity
- **City Density** — Total number of unique cities serviced or recorded.
- **Postal Code Reach** — The total count of unique postal codes signifying hyper-local data points.

## Dashboard Focus (Recommended)

### KPI Section
- **Total Unique Countries** — Summary count of all countries in the dataset.
- **Total Unique Regions** — Summary count of all broad geographical regions.

### Analysis Sections
- **Regional Composition** — Breakdown of states and cities categorized by their respective Region.
- **Location Granularity** — Distribution of postal codes mapped to cities and states to identify high-density areas.
