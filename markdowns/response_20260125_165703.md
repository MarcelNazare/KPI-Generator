## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Average Academic Stress Index** — Measures the overall self-reported stress level across the student population.
- **Average Competition Rating** — Quantifies the perceived intensity of academic competition among students.
- **Bad Habit Prevalence Rate** — Tracks the percentage of students engaging in daily smoking or drinking.

## 📈 Key Metrics
### Psychological & Social
- **Peer Pressure Level** — Gauges the intensity of social pressure experienced by students.
- **Home Academic Pressure** — Measures the level of performance expectations originating from the family environment.
- **Coping Strategy Distribution** — Identifies the most and least utilized methods for managing student stress.

### Environmental & Behavioral
- **Study Environment Quality** — Assesses the suitability and impact of the physical or social surroundings on learning.
- **Daily Bad Habit Frequency** — Monitors the regularity of substance use (smoking/drinking) among the cohort.

### Demographic & Time-based
- **Academic Stage Distribution** — Categorizes stress and behavior data by the student's current level of education.
- **Response Volume over Time** — Tracks the frequency of survey submissions using the timestamp data.

## Dashboard Focus (Recommended)

### KPI Section
- **Average Academic Stress Index** - High-level indicator of student mental well-being.
- **Bad Habit Ratio** - Percentage of students reporting daily smoking or drinking habits.
- **Competition Intensity** - Average rating of academic competition in the student life.

### Analysis Sections
- **Stress Index by Academic Stage** - Comparison of stress levels across different levels of education.
- **Coping Strategy Effectiveness** - Breakdown of stress index scores against specific coping mechanisms.
- **External Pressure Analysis** - Correlation between peer pressure, home pressure, and the stress index.
- **Environment Impact Study** - Comparison of stress levels across different study environments.
