## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Geographic Reach** — The total number of unique countries represented in the dataset.
- **Regional Footprint** — The count of distinct regions to measure broad territorial presence.

## 📈 Key Metrics
### Geographic Coverage
- **Unique State Count** — Total number of unique states or provinces covered by the data.
- **City Penetration** — Total count of unique cities where records are present.
- **Postal Code Density** — The total number of unique postal codes to identify hyper-local distribution.

### Operational Distribution
- **Region-to-Country Ratio** — The distribution of countries categorized within each specific region.
- **State Concentration per Region** — The volume of states managed within each regional boundary.

## Dashboard Focus (Recommended)

### KPI Section
- **Global Country Count** - Total unique countries in the portfolio.
- **Active Region Count** - Total number of active operational regions.

### Analysis Sections
- **Regional State Distribution** - Breakdown of states categorized by their respective regions.
- **City-Level Density Map** - Visualization of data points aggregated by city and postal code.
- **Postal Code Granularity** - Detailed view of specific localized areas within each state.
